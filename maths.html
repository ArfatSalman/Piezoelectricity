Although the above equations are the most used form in literature, some comments about the notation are necessary. Generally D and E are vectors, that is, Cartesian tensor of rank-1; and permittivity Îµ is Cartesian tensor of rank 2. Strain and stress are, in principle, also rank-2 tensors.
